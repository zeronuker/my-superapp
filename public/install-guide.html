· About the App ·
ClaudeBorne SuperApp is a free aviation utility app built by pilots, for pilots. It runs entirely on your phone — no account, no subscription, and no internet connection required for most functions. Everything is designed to be fast, clean, and usable in a cockpit environment. Once installed, it lives on your home screen and launches instantly, just like a native app.
· What's Inside ·
🔢
Basic & Scientific Calculator
Full-featured calculator with sin, cos, tan, log, √ and more. Switch between Basic and Scientific with one tap.
⏱
Time Calculator
Add, subtract, multiply and divide flight times in HH:MM format. Built for block time and duty calculations.
💱
Currency Converter
Live exchange rates with automatic offline fallback. Shows cached rate when no internet is available.
🌦
METAR / TAF Weather
Fetch weather for departure, arrival, destination alternates and enroute alternates. Color-coded by airport role. Cached for offline use.
📐
Interpolation Calculator
Linear and bilinear table interpolation for performance manual lookups.
✈
EDTO Performance
One-engine-inoperative drift-down altitude for Boeing 737 NG & MAX variants. Accounts for weight, ISA deviation and anti-ice.
⏰
FTL Calculator
Flight time limitation calculator based on CAD 1901 (CAAM Malaysia). Covers maximum FDP, standby duty, in-flight relief, split duty and PIC discretion for flight and cabin crew.
ℹ What is a PWA? A Progressive Web App works just like a regular app — it has its own icon on your home screen, opens without a browser bar, and works without an internet connection once installed. You do not need to go through the App Store or Google Play. Installation is free.
🤖  Android
Google Chrome · Recommended
1
Open Chrome and go to the website
Open the Chrome app on your Android phone. Tap the address bar at the top, type https://claudeborne-superapp.vercel.app and press Go on your keyboard.
2

· Common Questions ·
Does the app work without internet?
Yes. Once installed, all calculators work offline. The METAR/TAF weather section and currency converter require an internet connection for live data, but will show the last fetched data if you are offline.
Does it update automatically?
Yes. The app updates itself in the background whenever a new version is available. You do not need to reinstall — just close and reopen the app.
How do I uninstall it?
The same way you remove any app — press and hold the icon on your home screen, then select Remove or Delete.
Is it safe to install?
Yes. No data is collected or stored on any server. All your inputs stay on your own device. The app does not require any account or login.
I do not see "Add to Home Screen" anywhere.
On iPhone: make sure you are using Safari, not Chrome or another browser. On Android: make sure you are using Google Chrome. Other browsers may not support this feature.
